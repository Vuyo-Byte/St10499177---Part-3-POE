1.1	Brief History

AutoCare Service was established in 2026 as a startup initiative aimed at modernising the vehicle servicing industry through digital technology. The idea was developed after identifying the challenges many vehicle owners face when trying to book maintenance services through traditional garages.

Most local garages still depend on phone calls, walk-ins, and manual scheduling systems, which often create inconvenience, delays, and uncertainty for customers. AutoCare Service was created to simplify this process — providing a user-friendly online platform where customers can easily browse services, compare pricing, and schedule appointments with zero friction.

The website is currently under development and already includes detailed service descriptions, pricing information, and service package options designed to improve customer understanding and transparency.


1.2 Mission Statement
Mission	To simplify vehicle maintenance by providing customers with a convenient, transparent, and reliable digital servicing experience.

1.3 Vision Statement
Vision	To become one of South Africa's leading online vehicle service platforms, transforming how customers access and manage vehicle maintenance services.

3. MARKET CONTEXT & OPPORTUNITY


3.1 South African Digital Landscape

South Africa is one of Africa's most digitally active markets, with over 45 million internet users and a rapidly growing e-commerce and digital services sector. Smartphone penetration in urban centres like Durban continues to accelerate, with more consumers turning to mobile devices for everyday services including healthcare bookings, food delivery, and financial services.

Despite this digital momentum, the automotive servicing sector remains significantly behind the curve. The majority of independent garages and service centres in KwaZulu-Natal still operate on walk-in or phone-based booking systems, creating a clear and addressable gap in the market.

9. SEO & DIGITAL MARKETING STRATEGY


9.1 Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)
A robust SEO strategy is critical to ensuring AutoCare Service is discoverable by customers actively searching for vehicle servicing in Durban and the surrounding areas. The following on-page and local SEO initiatives will be implemented at launch:

SEO Initiative	Details
Keyword Strategy	Target: 'car service Durban', 'vehicle maintenance KZN', 'online car booking South Africa', 'affordable car service near me'
On-Page SEO	Optimised title tags, meta descriptions, header hierarchy (H1-H3), image alt text, and structured data markup on all pages
Local SEO	Google Business Profile setup and optimisation for Durban location — enables Google Maps visibility and local pack ranking
Page Speed	Compressed images, lazy loading, minified CSS/JS, and Cloudflare CDN — targeting sub-2.5 second load times
Sitemap & Indexing	XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console on launch day — all pages crawlable and indexed within 48 hours
Content Strategy	FAQ section and service descriptions written with target keywords naturally embedded to improve organic ranking over time
Backlink Building	Listings on local business directories (HelloPeter, Gumtree, Yellow Pages SA) to build domain authority from launch

14. POST-LAUNCH ROADMAP & SUCCESS METRICS


14.1 Post-Launch Growth Roadmap
The initial website launch is only the beginning. The following phased roadmap outlines the strategic enhancements planned beyond Week 10 to continuously improve performance and customer experience:

Phase	Initiative & Description
Month 1–2 (Stabilise)	Monitor Google Analytics daily. Fix any post-launch bugs. Respond to first customer bookings and gather feedback. Activate Google Business Profile.
Month 2–3 (Optimise)	Analyse bounce rate and session duration data. A/B test homepage CTA button placement. Begin targeted Facebook and Google Ads campaigns for Durban area.
Month 3–6 (Grow)	Launch customer testimonials and reviews section. Integrate WhatsApp chat. Begin email newsletter campaign with service reminders and seasonal offers.
Month 6–9 (Expand)	Implement online payment gateway (PayFast / Ozow). Add a loyalty or referral programme. Explore partnerships with local fleet vehicle operators.
Month 9–12 (Scale)	Evaluate expansion to additional service centres or partner garages. Launch a mobile app concept if booking volumes justify the investment.
